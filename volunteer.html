Volunteer information & Registration

HSS in collaboration with Hindu Heritage Centre temple(HHC) is organizing Seva program to deliver Prasad(Cooked Vegetarian Meal) for Hindu Students studying in Sheridan College - Davis campus, Brampton. We are looking for volunteers to help us in this Noble cause.

The ask for volunteers is to help with one or more of the below task every Wednesday around 6 PM for 2 to 3 Hours(Each registrant will be requested to volunteer 1 slot (2 to 3 hours) in 2 weeks or more depending on total number of volunteer registrations)

Help package the food at the Hindu Heritage Centre temple, Mississauga

Load the package to Vehicle

Distribute the package to other volunteers at a location in the Sheridan college campus

Distribute some of the package to registered students(Contactless delivery)

After you register below, we will contact you to go over details.If you would like more information please contact us at hsshhcseva@gmail.com
