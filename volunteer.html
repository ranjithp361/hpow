Volunteers info

HSS in collaboration with Hindu Heritage Centre temple(HHC) is organizing Seva program to deliver Prasad(Cooked Vegetarion Meal) to Hindu Students studying in Sheridan College - Davis campus, Brampton. The ask for volunteers is to help with one or more of the below task every Wednesday around 6 PM 1) Help package the food at the Hindu Heritage Centre temple, Mississauga 2) Load the package to Vehicle 3) Distribute the package to other volunteers at a location in the Sheridan college campus 4) Distribute some of the package to registered students(Contactless delivery) After you fill out this order request, we will contact you to go over details.If you would like more information please contact us at hsshhcseva@gmail.com.
