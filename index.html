Hindu Prasad On Wheels (HPOW)

Hindu Heritage Centre Mississauga and Hindu Swayam Sevak Sangh Canada Organizing Anna Daana(Free Food Delivery), and has partnered with Sheridan College in Brampton

Due to latest Provincial wide COVID restrictions for outdoor gathering, we are temporarily changing this program to Home delivery model. When you register with your address and email, we will deliver the food at your doorstep free of cost. Please note, we are doing it as Seva(Help without expecting anything in return) related to HHC temple and no strings attached. Its our best effort to serve the International student community and thank you for giving us an opportunity

Location

Free Home Delivery of food around Sheridan College - Davis Campus
